Smart DNS Study

A study on how users understand and perceive Smart DNS services.

About the Study

Researchers at Georgetown University and The George Washington University seek to learn how users of Smart DNS services understand and interact with them. Study participants will be asked to fill out an online questionnaire consisting of technical questions and questions about how they have (or plan to) set up and interact with Smart DNS services. We expect the survey to take about 15-20 minutes to complete.

Participate

Thank you for your interest in participating in the study. Please review the eligibility criteria to participate before continuing on to the questionnaire.

Participants who complete the 15-20 minute survey will be eligible to win a $50 Amazon gift card for their participation in this study.

Eligibility Criteria

Participants must:

1. Be at least 18 years of age

2. Fall into one of the following categories:

  • Participant has used a Smart DNS service in the past; or
  • Currently uses a Smart DNS service; or
  • Is seriously considering using a Smart DNS service within the next four weeks;

If you meet the above criteria, you can access the survey by clicking the button below.
